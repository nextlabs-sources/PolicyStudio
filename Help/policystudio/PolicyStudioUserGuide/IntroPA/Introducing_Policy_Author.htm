Introducing Policy Author

Implementing your information control policies involves using the Control Center Policy Studio to define a set of components that represent entities in your environment; using those components to construct policies; deploying those pol­icies to all relevant Control Center Policy Enforcers installed throughout the network; and managing and fine-tuning the policies when necessary, after they have been deployed.

To perform these functions, Policy Studio provides two powerful interfaces, Pol­icy Author and Policy Manager. This chapter provides an introduction to Policy Author, which is where you define your components and policies, and then gives a general description of the standard policy life cycle. Later chapters will dive more deeply into the details of defining components, constructing policies, and deploying them throughout your organization.
