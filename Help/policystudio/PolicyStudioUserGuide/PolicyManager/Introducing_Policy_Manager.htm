Introducing Policy Manager

The previous sections described how to use Policy Author to create and use com­ponents and policies. This section describes the features of Policy Studio’s other interface, Policy Manager. Policy Manager is where you monitor and manage components and policies once they have been designed and built in Policy Author. For a quick reference to many of the features of Policy Manager, see How Do I...?

The section covers the following topics:

·        Using Policy Manager

·        Importing and Exporting Policies

·        Monitoring Objects

Note: In this section, the generic terms object or policy object are often used to refer to components and policies together, whenever the dis­cussion applies to both equally.
