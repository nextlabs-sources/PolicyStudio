Using the Sample Policy Sets

In this chapter we will present several use cases that provide illustrations of the way you can define sets of policies to solve specific business problems. The pol­icies and components for these cases are predefined, and after you install Policy Studio they are available in the policy tree of both Policy Author and Policy Man­ager, for you to view and experiment with.
