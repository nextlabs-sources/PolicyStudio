The SIG Reporting Module

The SharePoint Information Governance (SIG) Reporting Module delivers predefined, preformatted reports on how SharePoint data is being used across the enterprise, broken down by user, department, business process, lifecycle stage, and similar useful categories that can help analysts and auditors as they design and adjust SharePoint access policies.

This chapter provides a general overview of how the module works and instructions for setting it up and writing policies that take advantage of its features. The chapter is divided into the following sections:
